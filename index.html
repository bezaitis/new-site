Student - University of Illinois @ Urbana-Champaign

Evanston, IL

Hi! My name is John Bezaitis, a Junior at the University of Illinois @ Urbana-Champaign majoring in Economics and Statistics. Recently, I interned at Cerity Partners, a wealth management firm based in downtown Chicago. I am currently pursing my next opportunity in the world of finance, and can't wait to learn more!

On campus, I act as senior analyst for UIUC's Investment Management Academy, a student run portfolio benchmarked against the S&P 600. I am the president of the Euchre League, a card playing club dedicated to the game 'Euchre'. Otherwise, I serve as a mentor for the Illini Mentor Program, a volunteer program that dedicates time to local elementary school students as an after school program.

In my free time, you can find me sailing, playing cards, traveling, and watching the Chicago Bears! I have a dog named Poppy who is the cutest puppy ever, you can see some photos of her here. Thanks for visiting my site, and be sure to check out my trips to see what I've been up to as of late!
